About

Web App for Climate Change Awareness



↓

Motivation

Climate change is one of our biggest and most dangerous problems, but our lack of awareness is slowing down our attempt to tackle it.

If people knew that climate change would affect them as an individual, and how, people would be more inclined to become mobilized and join the many people that believe we can live in harmony with ourselves and our planet.



↓

Globe

This is the home page of the web app, and is available by clicking Globe on the navigation bar.

Rotate the globe and select different markers to view statements which have happened, or have been predicted to happen in the future.

The statements convey effects and causes of climate change.

Underneath each statement their is a link to a page which gives more information backing up the statement.



↓

More Information

By clicking the link underneath a statement, you can find out more about the statement.

By scrolling down, the first section you come to gives you more information surrounding the statement.

The next section gives highlights the significance of climate change as it relates to the statement.

After that, you can find out where the information given to you was found, so you can do some reading into the topic for yourself if you would like to.

And finally, by scrolling to the bottom, you are taken to the section from which you can access the opportunities page so you can find out how you can fight climate change.



↓

Opportunities

By clicking the button in the opportunities section of a more information page, or by clicking Opportunities on the navigation bar, you can access the page which gives information on how you can fight climate change.

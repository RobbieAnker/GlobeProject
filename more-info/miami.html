An Impact of Climate Change

Miami Dade county is one of many victims of global sea water level rise. The sea water level has risen and will continue to rise unless climate change stops.

One way that the warming climate has caused this is by melting glaciers and ice sheets. Also, the sea water has expanded due to its increased temperature.

An Impact of Climate Change

This year's Austrlian bushfires have been more extreme than those of the past.

Bushfires are caused by the presence of dry plant life which gives fuel for the fire. The recent bushfires were so intense because it followed an immense drought which provided an abundance of dry fuel for the bushfires. Climate change makes Australia more at risk of drought due to the increased temperature, and in turn more at risk of bushfires.

As Antarctica gets warmer, its glaciers melt into the sea. This causes the sea level to rise which puts coastal areas at risk of flooding.

An Impact of Climate Change

The warming of Antarctica is one of the most clear signs that the planet is heating up.

The Antarctic Peninsula has already experienced air temperature increases of 3 °C, which is 5 times more than the earth's average level of warming.
